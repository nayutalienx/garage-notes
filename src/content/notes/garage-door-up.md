## What I Am Trying To Do With This Blog

This blog is a public lab notebook. The goal is to publish useful intermediate thinking instead of waiting until every topic becomes a polished article.

## Small Notes Over Perfect Essays

Most engineering work produces fragments: a failed approach, a command that finally worked, a design question that needs more testing, or a short explanation that would be useful later. Those fragments should have a place.

## Public Version vs Private Version

The public note should keep the reasoning and remove the private material. Internal hostnames, credentials, customer details, banking information, private chats, and unfinished legal or business details stay out.

## Avoid Leaking Secrets

Before publishing, check for tokens, passwords, API keys, IPs that should not be public, private infrastructure endpoints, and copy-paste instructions that would help abuse a live system.

## Next Step

Use notes for small discoveries, then promote the useful ones into blog posts when the shape becomes stable.
